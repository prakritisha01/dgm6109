Screen Time and Sleep Quality (High Screen Time Nights)

Question: When the time spent on the screen prior to sleep is more than 60 minutes, is there a reduction in the quality of sleep?

In this visualization, the horizontal axis is occupied by screen time and the quality of sleep is put on the vertical axis. The dataset is filtered to keep only screen time more than 60 minutes.
Triangles indicate total sleep time. Night with 7 or more hours of sleep are depicted with upward triangles and nights with less than 7 hours of sleep are depicted with a downward triangle.
