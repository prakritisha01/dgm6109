Screen Time and Sleep Quality

Question: Does sleep quality decrease on nights when screen time before bed is higher than 60 minutes?

About the Visualizatiion

In this Scatterplot, the horizontal axis is shows screen time and the quality of sleep is shown on the vertical axis. A red dashed line at 60 min helps seperate lower and higher screen-time nights.

Additional Data Shown

Triangles indicate total sleep time. Night with 7 or more hours of sleep are depicted with upward triangles and nights with less than 7 hours of sleep are depicted with a downward triangle.

Patterns and Conclusion

This graph indicates that sleep quality tends to be at a low level on those nights when the screen time before bed is higher, particularly after 60 minutes. There are quite a few high screen-time nights towards the lower part of the sleep- quality scale. But it is not the ideal relationship, since there are still some nights where there is a better sleep quality despite the increased screen time. Generally, according to the visualization, there is an inverse correlation between screen time before sleep and quality of sleep.
